# Referências

## Normas e modelos

- ISO/IEC 25012. *Data Quality Model*.
- ISO 8000. *Data Quality and Enterprise Master Data*.
- DAMA International. *DAMA-DMBOK: Data Management Body of Knowledge*.

## Livros

- REDMAN, Thomas C. *Data Driven: Profiting from Your Most Important Business Asset*. Harvard Business Review Press, 2008.
- McGILVRAY, Danette. *Executing Data Quality Projects*. Morgan Kaufmann, 2008.
- REIS, Joe; HOUSLEY, Matt. *Fundamentals of Data Engineering*. O'Reilly Media, 2022.
- KLEPPMANN, Martin. *Designing Data-Intensive Applications*. O'Reilly Media, 2017.

## Documentação técnica

- Great Expectations. *Expectations and Data Docs*.
- Amazon Web Services. *Deequ — Unit Tests for Data*.
- dbt Labs. *Data Tests and Contracts*.
- OpenLineage. *Open Standard for Data Lineage*.
- OpenTelemetry. *Logs, Metrics and Traces*.
