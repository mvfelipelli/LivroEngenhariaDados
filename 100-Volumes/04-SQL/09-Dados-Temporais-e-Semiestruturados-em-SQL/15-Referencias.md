## Documentação oficial e padrões

- PostgreSQL Global Development Group. [Range Types](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/current/rangetypes.html).
- PostgreSQL Global Development Group. [JSON Types](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/current/datatype-json.html).
- PostgreSQL Global Development Group. [JSON Functions and Operators](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/current/functions-json.html).
- SQLite Project. [JSON Functions and Operators](https://www.sqlite.org/json1.html).
- IANA. [Time Zone Database](https://www.iana.org/time-zones).
- ISO. *ISO 8601 — Date and time representations*.
